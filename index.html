MEET THE GURU

Guided Wisdom

Jaggu Ji, a revered spiritual guide with over two decades of experience, is known for his profound insights into spirituality, meditation, and yoga.

His teachings emphasize inner peace, self-realization, and the holistic integration of body, mind, and spirit.

Through his wisdom, Jaggu Ji has inspired countless individuals in Yoga Ashram, on their journeys toward self-discovery and enlightenment.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect in my first yoga class?

Expect a gentle introduction to basic poses, breathing exercises, and relaxation techniques.

What are the visiting hours for the ashram?

Visitors are welcome between 9:00 AM and 6:00 PM(IST). Please check in with the front desk upon arrival.

Do I need to register in advance to visit?

Day visits don’t require registration, but overnight stays and special events may require prior registration.

Do you offer beginner classes?

Yes, we have classes for all levels, including beginners.

Can I stay overnight at the ashram?

Yes, we offer accommodation options for guests. Contact us in advance to check availability.
